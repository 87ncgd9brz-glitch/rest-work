Our Story

Welcome to PHAKAtwayo Culinary Solutions, where tradition meets innovation. Our passion is bringing the rich, vibrant flavors of African cuisine to your table.

Phaka Twayo Culinary solutions is a culinary group founded by chef Mabaya also known as Chef Cook. This culinary group aims to provide hospitality especially culinary related services and solutions. The group company will run several american style fast food cafes, private and family cheffing services, catering for small and intimate events, menu planning and consultation services for start up restaurants, cafes and fast food restaurants. Basic Culinary training services will also be available for start up restaurants. Through the upcoming years we aim to grow to be Africas leading culinary solutions and hospitality¬†group.
